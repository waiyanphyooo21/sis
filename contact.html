Our Story

Sisburma is a men’s fashion brand founded in 2016 with a vision to create a leading brand originally born in Burma and to offer young people a better lifestyle. We believe that fashion is more than just clothing — it’s a way of expressing oneself and making a statement. That’s why we create unique, classic, and smart pieces that combine style, comfort, and functionality.


Today, Sisburma is proud to offer a range of men’s clothing and accessories that embody our brand values of uniqueness, classicism, and smartness. Our designs are inspired by the international trending patterns and fabrics that have been passed down through generations, but with a contemporary twist that makes them fresh and modern.

We are committed to using high-quality materials and ethical manufacturing practices to create products that are not only stylish but also durable and sustainable. We believe in offering our customers value for money, which is why we keep our prices affordable without compromising on quality.



At Sisburma, we’re not just a fashion brand — we’re a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for style, creativity, and culture. We invite you to join us on our journey and experience the beauty and uniqueness of Sisburma.

About Us

“Sisburma is a men’s clothing brand based in Burma, with stores located in modern trade shopping centers across the country, including City Mall, Junction City, Capital Mall Yangon, and Capital Mall Mandalay. Our vision is to create a leading lifestyle brand offering sustainable, socially responsible practices and adapting to customers’ needs. We value excellence and are committed to being an effective, zero-tolerance workplace.”
